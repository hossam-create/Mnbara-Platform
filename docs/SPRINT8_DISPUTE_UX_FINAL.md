# SPRINT 8: DISPUTE UX & USER TRUST COPY (FINAL)

## 1️⃣ Entry Point — "Report an Issue"

### Title
**We're here to help**

### Subtext
If something didn't go as expected, you can tell us what happened.
We'll guide you step by step.

### Primary CTA
**Start issue review**

### Secondary CTA
**I just need information**

---

## 2️⃣ Issue Selection Screen

### Prompt
**What best describes the issue?**

### Options (examples)
- [ ] I didn't receive the item
- [ ] The item is different from what was agreed
- [ ] Delivery was delayed
- [ ] I'm unsure and want help reviewing
- [ ] Something else

### Helper Text
Choose the option that feels closest. You can explain more next.

---

## 3️⃣ Evidence Request (Minimal & Calm)

### Title
**Help us understand the situation**

### Copy
To review this fairly, we may ask for a few details or documents.
Only what's relevant — nothing more.

### Examples Shown Inline
- Photos of the item (if applicable)
- Messages related to the agreement
- Delivery or pickup confirmation (if available)

### Reassurance
This information is only used for this review.

---

## 4️⃣ Review in Progress State

### Title
**We're reviewing your case**

### Status Explanation (Bullet Points)
- ✓ Your submission was received
- ✓ A human reviewer is checking the details
- ✓ No action has been taken yet

### Expectation Setting
Most reviews take 24–72 hours.
You'll be notified if we need anything else.

---

## 5️⃣ "What Happens Next?" Panel (Always Visible)

### Step-by-Step Timeline
1. **We review the information**
   A trained team member looks at the details and context.

2. **We may ask a follow-up question**
   Only if something is unclear.

3. **A decision is suggested**
   Based on the facts and platform rules.

4. **You can respond or appeal**
   If you think something was missed.

### Footer Note
Decisions are based on evidence and policy — never automatic.

---

## 6️⃣ Outcome Explanation (Any Result)

### If Resolved in User's Favor
Based on the information provided, we found that your concern is valid.
Here's what we recommend next.

### If Not Fully Supported
We couldn't confirm the issue based on the available information.
This doesn't mean your concern isn't important.

### Always Include
- What information was considered
- Why this outcome was suggested
- What the user can do next

---

## 7️⃣ Appeal Option (Non-Confrontational)

### Title
**Want us to take another look?**

### Copy
If you believe something important was missed, you can request a second review.

### CTA
**Request another review**

### Note
A different reviewer will look at the case.

---

## 8️⃣ Language Rules (Enforced Globally)

### ✅ Use:
- "Review"
- "Suggestion"
- "Based on the information"
- "You can choose"
- "Here's what happens next"

### ❌ Never use:
- "Violation"
- "Penalty"
- "Legal action"
- "Final decision"
- "You must"

---

## 9️⃣ Trust Reinforcement Footer (All Dispute Screens)

Our role is to help both sides reach a fair outcome.
We don't take automatic actions, and every case is reviewed by a human.

---

## ✅ Status

- Calm tone ✔️
- Step-by-step clarity ✔️
- "What happens next" always visible ✔️
- No threatening language ✔️
- No legal intimidation ✔️
