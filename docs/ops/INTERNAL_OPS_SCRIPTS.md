# Internal Ops Scripts

Short, reusable scripts for common situations. Use these when you're under pressure.

---

## 1. "We Are Investigating"

### Standard Investigation

**Chat/Email:**
> We're looking into this now. I'll update you within [TIME].

**Ticket Note:**
> Investigating. ETA: [TIME].

---

### With Acknowledgment

**Chat/Email:**
> Got it. We're on it. You'll hear from us within [TIME].

**If user is frustrated:**
> I hear you. We're looking into this right now. I'll update you within [TIME], even if we don't have a full answer yet.

---

### For Escalated Issues

> This has been escalated. Someone senior is reviewing it now. We'll update you within [TIME].

### For System Issues

## 2. "We Cannot Assist With This"

### Outside Our Scope

**Chat/Email:**
> This isn't something we can help with directly, but here's what might work: [ALTERNATIVE].

**If no alternative:**
> This falls outside what we can do. I'm sorry we can't help here.

---

### Policy Limitation

**Chat/Email:**
> I understand this is frustrating. Unfortunately, we can't [ACTION] because [SHORT_REASON]. Here's what we can do instead: [ALTERNATIVE].

**If no alternative:**
> I wish I could help with this, but it's not something we're able to do. I'm sorry.

---

### Legal/Compliance Block

**Chat/Email:**
> We're not able to [ACTION] for legal/compliance reasons. I can't go into detail, but this isn't something we can change.

**Internal Note:**
> Declined per [POLICY]. Do not override.

---

### Account Restriction

**Chat/Email:**
> Your account has a restriction that prevents [ACTION]. If you think this is a mistake, reply and we'll review it.

**If permanent:**
> This restriction is permanent and can't be reversed. I'm sorry.

---

### Third-Party Issue

**Chat/Email:**
> This is happening on [THIRD_PARTY]'s side, not ours. You'll need to contact them directly. Here's how: [CONTACT_INFO].

---

## 3. "Here's What Happens Next"

### Standard Next Steps

**Chat/Email:**
> Here's what happens next:
> 1. [STEP_1]
> 2. [STEP_2]
> 3. [STEP_3]
>
> Timeline: [ESTIMATE]

---

### After Dispute Filed

**Chat/Email:**
> Here's what happens now:
> 1. We review what you sent (24-48 hours)
> 2. We contact the other party
> 3. We make a decision and let you know
>
> Your payment stays protected the whole time.

---

### After Refund Approved

**Chat/Email:**
> Your refund is approved.
> - Amount: [AMOUNT]
> - Method: Back to your original payment
> - Timeline: 3-5 business days
>
> You'll get a confirmation email when it's processed.

---

### After Account Action

**Chat/Email:**
> We've [ACTION] your account.
> - What changed: [CHANGE]
> - Why: [REASON]
> - What you can do: [OPTIONS]

---

### After Escalation

**Chat/Email:**
> I've escalated this to our [TEAM] team.
> - They'll review within [TIME]
> - You'll hear directly from them
> - Reference: [TICKET_ID]

---

### Waiting on User

**Chat/Email:**
> We need [ITEM] from you to move forward. Once we have it, we'll [NEXT_STEP] within [TIME].

---

### Issue Resolved

**Chat/Email:**
> This is resolved.
> - What we did: [ACTION]
> - Result: [OUTCOME]
>
> Let us know if anything else comes up.

---

## Quick Combos

### Investigating + Next Steps

> We're looking into this. Here's what happens:
> 1. We investigate (next [TIME])
> 2. We update you with findings
> 3. We resolve or explain options

---

### Can't Help + Alternative

> We can't [ACTION], but here's what might work: [ALTERNATIVE]. Want me to help with that instead?

---

### Resolved + Follow-up

> Done. [SUMMARY]. If this happens again, [PREVENTION_TIP].

---

## Stress Shortcuts

When you're slammed, use these:

| Situation | Script |
|-----------|--------|
| Need more time | "Looking into this. Update in [TIME]." |
| Can't help | "Can't do that one. Sorry." |
| Done | "Done. Let me know if anything else." |
| Need info | "Need [X] to continue." |
| Escalating | "Escalated. You'll hear back within [TIME]." |

---

## Don't Say

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| "Unfortunately, per our policy..." | "We can't do that because..." |
| "I'm just following procedure" | "Here's why..." |
| "There's nothing I can do" | "Here's what I can do..." |
| "You should have..." | "Next time, try..." |
| "That's not my department" | "Let me get you to the right team" |
| "I don't know" | "Let me find out" |
