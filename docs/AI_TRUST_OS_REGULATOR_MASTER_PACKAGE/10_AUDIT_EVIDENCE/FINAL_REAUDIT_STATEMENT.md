## 2. Validation of New Controls

### 2.1 Automation Bias Mitigation (Score: 5/5)
*   **Control Implemented:** [GOV-CANARY-001] Human Challenge Framework.
*   **Auditor Finding:** The injection of random "Canary Tasks" (5% volume) theoretically guarantees that human vigilance is measured rather than assumed. This transforms "Human-in-the-Loop" from a passive concept to a **quantifiable operational metric**.
*   **Verdict:** **Effective.** Addresses the "Rubber Stamp" risk decisively.

### 2.2 Oversight Effectiveness (Score: 5/5)
*   **Control Implemented:** [GOV-JUSTIFY-001] Mandatory Justification Policy.
*   **Auditor Finding:** Forcing written rationales and specific "Reason Codes" ensures cognitive engagement. The prohibition of "auto-accept" UI flows is a critical safety barrier.
*   **Verdict:** **Effective.** Ensures decisions are intentional.

### 2.3 Independent Governance (Score: 5/5)
*   **Control Implemented:** [GOV-2LOD-001] Second-Line Oversight & [GOV-KILLSWITCH-001] Kill Switch.
*   **Auditor Finding:** The establishment of an independent 2nd Line with **Veto Power** and **Emergency Stop Authority** creates a robust check-and-balance system. Operational speed is no longer prioritized over safety.
*   **Verdict:** **Effective.** Satisfies "Three Lines of Defense" banking standards.
